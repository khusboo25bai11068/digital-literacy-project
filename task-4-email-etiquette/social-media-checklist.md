The Do's (Responsible Use)
Do set your accounts to private: Keep your personal photos and family details visible only to people you actually know.

Do think before you post: Ask yourself if you would be comfortable with a future employer or your professor seeing the post.

Do verify news before sharing: Use a quick Google search to check if a "viral" message or news story is authentic before hitting forward.

Do use strong, unique passwords: Use a different password for every platform and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

Do credit original creators: If you are sharing someone's art, photo, or research, always mention their handle or name.



The Don'ts (Avoid These)
Don't post sensitive documents: Never share photos of your Hall Ticket, Aadhaar card, or Internship offer letters.

Don't engage with trolls: Avoid getting into heated arguments or using abusive language in comment sections; digital footprints are permanent.

Don't share your live location: Avoid posting "Current Location" stories while you are still at the venue. Post them after you leave.

Don't click on suspicious links: Avoid random "Check who visited your profile" or "Win a free gift" links sent via DMs.

Don't overshare personal venting: Avoid posting deep personal or internal college conflicts that could be taken out of context later.
